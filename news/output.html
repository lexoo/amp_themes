New annual return templates for businesses with employee share schemes

Rebecca Routledge Apr 21, 2016

HMRC has released new templates for businesses to complete their annual returns regarding employment shares and securities.

Available on HMRC's website, the 2015-16 versions of the forms are very similar to the ones organisations were required to fill in last year.

Businesses that operate employee share schemes or reward members of staff with securities have until 6 July 216 to complete and submit the new documents.

However, the system crashed as the deadline approached last year, so companies may be well-advised to give themselves plenty of breathing space.

There has been criticism from some quarters that changes to the Enterprise Management Incentive template are inconsistent.

Any queries regarding the new forms should be raised with HMRC.
